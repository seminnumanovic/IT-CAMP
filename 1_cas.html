About Novi Pazar

Novi Pazar is a city located in Serbia, in the southwest part of the country. It is the administrative center of the Raška District and has a rich cultural and historical heritage.

Landmarks and Attractions

  • Ras Fortress: A medieval fortress situated on a hill overlooking the city.
  • Djeravica Peak: The highest peak in the region, offering stunning views and hiking opportunities.
  • Altun-Alem Mosque: A beautiful Ottoman-era mosque known for its unique architectural style.
  • Narodni Muzej: The National Museum of Novi Pazar, showcasing artifacts from the city's history.

Local Cuisine

When in Novi Pazar, make sure to try some delicious local dishes, such as ćevapi (grilled meat sausages), burek (flaky pastry filled with meat or cheese), and kajmak (a creamy dairy product).
